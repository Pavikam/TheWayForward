Home

Hello, and a warm welcome! Or a cooler welcome, if you find the weather too warm! In this space of the web world, we discuss life. That’s the most simplest way to put it. The Way Forward tries to figure out how to live better, healthier and wholly. We address some of the societal issues that curb the beauty that life is, we address how our own self can be a barrier to a beautiful life, we try to find the path through these jungles and yet not lose the zest for admiration, and many ‘so ons’! Tour through our writings in the Perceptions menu and you’ll get an idea what we mean by this. Join one of our Webinar events to get a better idea of what goes on here. (There is a little about us over here).

Make yourself comfortable, because The Way Forward is not always comfortable in the beginning; but we all want to journey forwards (in whatever terms ‘forward’ is to us!)…

If you find that you can relate to our perspectives, contribute, or have anything good/bad to say at all, please do. In anyways, you are welcome to join us in this Way Forward.
